Services &
Pricing

Custom photography & video for students, athletes, and creators.

Photography Packages

All packages include both edited photos and original files. Prices are approximate and depend on customization, time, and project needs.

Package What You Get Price Range (USD)
Basic 20–40 edited photos + originals, light retouching. $20–$40
Standard 40–80 edited photos + originals, color grading & basic retouching. $50–$80
Premium 80–120 edited photos + originals, detailed retouching & creative edits. $90–$120
Pro / Event Up to ~150 edited photos + originals, event coverage or custom project. $130–$150

Video Production

I also offer video production, including highlights, short reels, and simple story-style edits. Pricing is discussed case by case, depending on length, editing complexity, and shooting time. If you have an idea, just reach out and we can plan it together.

Free Sample

Not sure yet? You can try a free sample edit first. Send a few photos, and I’ll edit a small set. If you like the style, we move forward with a full package.

Free Projects

If your idea is something fun and creative, like nature, travel, or a personal passion project, I’m often happy to shoot or edit for free, depending on schedule. I love working on projects that tell good stories or explore interesting places.

Gear & Tools

To keep the quality high, I shoot and edit with a mix of personal and professional equipment:

  • Sony A6400 camera
  • 70–300mm G Master lens
  • Sigma fast prime lenses (e.g. 55mm, 22mm)
  • DJI RSC2 stabilizer / gimbal
  • Portable “human” lights for portraits and low light

Editing & Post-Production

  • Adobe Lightroom – color grading & batch photo editing
  • Adobe Photoshop – detailed retouching & composites
  • Adobe Premiere Pro – video editing & storytelling
  • Adobe After Effects (if needed) – motion graphics & special effects

SOJC Equipment Access

As a graduate student at the University of Oregon School of Journalism and Communication, I also have access to SOJC’s equipment room, including professional lights, audio gear, and studio spaces, which can be used for higher-end shoots when needed.
